Three Kinds of Directives in Angular:

We have three kind of directives available in Angular like below

  • Structural Directives: By using this directives we can change or Manipulate DOM by adding or removing the element
  • Attributes Directives: Changing the behavior and appearance of the specific Element
  • Component Directives: Component is also one kind of directives with Template and view
